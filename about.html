Hi, I am Kseniia. As you may deduct, from the big screaming heading of my page. I am a Web & Graphic Artist, addicted to perfect color matches and maniac for details. I found myself more and more involved into storytelling process using different things for it. Video, writing, coding and...This video is from my last trip to Paris. I fall in love with the city. I believe that every city adds up to us, its like little pieces of universe combining together, developing that one representations.
